About WOM-nology

WOM-nology was created by Brittany Coleman as a safe space for womxn within the tech industry.

WOM-nology derived as a project concept while studing at General Assembly as a Software Engineering Immersive Bootcamp Student. She immediately knew that she wanted to garner a community within the tech space, particularly amongst other womxn in the industry. project assignment while completing the Software Engineering Immersive program at General Assembly. As someone new to the technology space Brittany sought to create a community for herself amongst other womxn in the industry hence the creation of WOM-nology.

Brittany is a Full-Stack Developer from Atlanta, GA. She is also a wife and mother of two gorgeous girls ages 2 and 3.

Welcome to WOM-nology!
