About WOM-nology

Founder: Brittany Coleman

Welcome to WOM-nology!

An idea that began as a capstone project idea that has involved overtime into a community space for womxn. The aim of WOM-nology is to establish a community amongst womxn in technology that is both inclusive and intersectional. This community was created as a safe space for womxn to engage in conversation about issues that matter to them in and out of tech.

From our founder, thank you for being apart of WOM-nology!
